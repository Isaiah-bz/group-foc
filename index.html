Fundamental of Computing

About Course


This course is an introduction to the fundamentals of Computer Science that will provide students with a foundation for future courses in Computer Science. The course is a broad survey of the discipline through topics that allow students to understand the importance of computing in modern life and the ethical responsibilities of computer professionals. You will learn about core topics like algorithms, which are step-by-step procedures for solving problems. The course also teaches you how computers represent all information, such as text and images, using binary notation (zeros and ones). Additionally, you will study how computers work inside. This includes understanding the main parts like the CPU and memory, and how the Internet and networks function globally.
